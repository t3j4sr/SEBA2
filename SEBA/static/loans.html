Smart Borrowing Tips
🔢Check CIBIL first. Score 750+ gets you the best rates. Check free at CIBIL.com or PaisaBazaar.
⚖️Keep EMI ≤ 40% of income. Banks allow up to 50% but 40% leaves breathing room for emergencies.
🚫Don't apply to multiple banks at once. Each inquiry drops your CIBIL score by 5–10 points.
💸Prepay when possible. Even ₹10K extra on principal per year reduces your tenure significantly.
📊Compare before signing. Even 0.5% lower rate = significant savings over long tenures. Use BankBazaar or MyLoanCare.
📄Education loan interest (80E) is 100% tax deductible with no ceiling — the best tax benefit available.
